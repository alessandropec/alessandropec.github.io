A.D.A.M.O. (**A**gent for language-**D**riven **A**ctions with **M**ultimodal **O**bservations) is a **visual-symbolic framework for language-driven virtual humans**: it combines **VLM perception** with **symbolic reasoning and action**, letting a virtual human ground natural-language instructions in what it actually perceives in the 3D environment.

I am currently extending the framework with **long-term spatial memory** for deep exploration of 3D environments, so that the agent can accumulate and reuse spatial knowledge over time instead of re-discovering the scene at every session.

The framework is presented in {% cite pecora2026adamo %}.
